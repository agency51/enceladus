Case Study

AI Strategy & Opportunity Discovery for a Leading Magazine Publisher

Immediate Media is one of the UK's leading specialist content publishers, with a diverse portfolio of well-known consumer magazine brands spanning lifestyle, knowledge, hobbies, and entertainment, across both print and digital channels.

The Challenge

Like many publishers, Immediate Media recognised that AI had moved rapidly from experimentation to genuine commercial opportunity — but the pace of change created uncertainty.

Senior leaders were asking fundamental questions:

  • Where can AI realistically add value across editorial, commercial, marketing, and operations?
  • How should AI be approached differently for digital products versus print workflows?
  • Which opportunities represent quick wins and which require deeper strategic investment?
  • How can innovation be pursued without compromising editorial quality, brand trust, or regulatory compliance?

Immediate Media engaged Enceladus to provide clarity, structure, and practical direction — cutting through hype to identify where AI could be deployed safely and effectively.

Our Approach

Enceladus delivered a senior-level AI awareness and opportunity discovery consultancy, designed specifically for the needs of a modern magazine publisher.

The engagement combined strategic insight with hands-on, practical examples, covering:

The AI Inflection Point

Why 2025–26 represents a shift from experimentation to embedded AI systems — and what this means for publishers.

AI Capability Landscape

A clear explanation of generative AI, copilots, retrieval-based systems, and emerging agentic workflows, grounded in real-world publishing use cases.

Opportunity Mapping Across the Publishing Value Chain

Identifying where AI could support:

  • Editorial research and content ideation
  • Audience insight and personalisation
  • Subscription growth and retention
  • Advertising and commercial operations
  • Print production and back-office efficiency

Online vs Offline Considerations

Exploring how AI opportunities differ between digital platforms (web, apps, newsletters, subscriptions) and print-led workflows (commissioning, layout, production, rights, and archives).

Governance, Ethics, and Trust

Addressing responsible AI use, IP protection, data privacy, and the importance of human editorial oversight — particularly critical in trusted media brands.

Rather than prescribing technology, the focus was on decision-making frameworks — enabling leaders to evaluate AI opportunities confidently and consistently.

The Solution

Enceladus delivered a tailored AI strategy and opportunity framework that Immediate Media could apply across its brands and functions.

Key Outputs

  • A clear taxonomy of AI opportunities — from low-risk productivity gains to higher-impact strategic initiatives
  • Practical examples of publisher-specific AI use cases, both live today and emerging over the next 2–3 years
  • Guidance on build vs buy vs partner decisions
  • A roadmap showing how AI adoption can evolve responsibly over time
  • Alignment with regulatory and ethical expectations relevant to media organisations

Crucially, the consultancy equipped leadership teams with a shared language and mental model for AI — accelerating internal conversations and decision-making.

The Impact

The engagement helped Immediate Media:

Move from abstract interest in AI to concrete, prioritised opportunities

Understand where AI can augment editorial excellence, not undermine it

Identify early wins that improve efficiency without cultural disruption

Build confidence in making informed AI investment decisions

Rather than chasing trends, Immediate Media gained a pragmatic, publisher-first view of AI — rooted in commercial value, brand trust, and operational reality.
